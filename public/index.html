While you are 'safe' now, it's important to understand the context of benzodiazepine prescription.

Why are they prescribed?

For insomnia, pain, anxiety, digestion, muscle spasm, ADHD

  • Over 100,000,000 million worldwide are prescribed a benzodiazepine
  • Between 10% and 15% of those prescribed long term will experience serious or fatal injury.
  • They are widely prescribed in almost every industrialised country

Injured victims can be properly supported, and we can gain greater trust in our medical systems.
